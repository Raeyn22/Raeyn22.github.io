About Supasoko

Supasoko was founded in 2023 with a mission to empower African farmers by connecting them directly to buyers and markets through technology.

Our platform eliminates middlemen, provides transparent pricing, and helps farmers get fair value for their hard work while ensuring buyers receive fresh, high-quality produce.

Currently operating in Nigeria, we plan to expand across West Africa to serve more farming communities.
